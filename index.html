TECHNICAL CONSULTANCY

Technical Expertise

Soltech Energy installs the Solar PV System at the premises of customer. The solar PV system installed at client premises is NOT owned by the customer but by a third party or Soltech Energy which is also responsible for its maintenance. However, there would be an agreement with that party and client to consume and purchase the power generated by Solar PV system. The agreement would be called as Power Purchase Agreement and pursue for a long period of 5 to 20 years. Thus, under PPA, a third party shall install Solar system at your premises and maintain it, you have to purchase energy produced by it at predetermined tariff in agreement.

PROJECT FINANCING

Capex Model

- The recent solar policies from the indian government and our state governments have made sure the availability of solar solutions by providing solar panels at cheaper rates. Capex refers to expenditure by consumer only. When customer choose CAPEX model to install solar system, he pay for complete installation and commissioning of the system. Consumer itself owns the system and also responsible for its maintenance. The energy produced by solar system is for own use of the customer. After Soltech Energy installs the solar power plant, customer takes the plant ownership. After installation, our engineers will ensure that the solar system installed run smoothly, efficiently and effectively under an operations and maintenance agreement.
